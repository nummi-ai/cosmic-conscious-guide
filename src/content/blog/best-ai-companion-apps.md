### Red Flags to Avoid

Be cautious of AI companions that:
- Make medical diagnoses or treatment recommendations
- Encourage isolation from real relationships
- Claim to solve serious mental health issues alone
- Don't have clear privacy policies
- Push excessive monetization

### Healthy AI Companion Use

- Complement, don't replace, human relationships
- Use as a tool for growth, not escape
- Maintain perspective on AI limitations
- Seek professional help for serious issues
- Balance digital and in-person interactions
